Viper

When discrimination against Asians began, Nick helped his friends Tongyi Shen and Jonathan Pan seek refuge in Chinatown. Here they happened upon a hidden Chinese syndicate, the Longying. Despite his race Nick was allowed to join their cause and he spent years training there with masters of ancient Chinese fighting techniques, in particular Changquan and the use of rope darts. During this time he lived in an apartment in Chinatown and trained as a lawyer, having long had an interest in the profession.

However, as he worked in the business corruption became very evident and Nick was appalled. Over this time the Longying became more powerful and conditions across the rest of Sydney worsened. Eventually Nick became so distraught with the state of the legal system he stopped his training to commit to his profession.
While he was away a US raid crippled the Longying and Nick became furious.
With the help of close friends, he put together his arsenal and left to become one of the first vigilantes, Viper. He continues to work as a lawyer.

Vigilantes by Hunter, Siena, Nicholas.
